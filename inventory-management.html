PROCESS 2 & 3 Sales Channels — OTC (Branch) & E-Commerce

🏪 Over-the-Counter (OTC) — Branch Sales
OTC 1
Customer Arrives — Product Enquiry
Staff checks live stock availability in WinGold for that branch. Presents products at live WinGold pricing.
Manual
OTC 2
KYC Verification (GBG)
KYC via Gold Secure GBG (integrated with WinGold). New customers registered; returning customers looked up. AML compliance checks completed. Transaction proceeds only on KYC clearance.
Compliance
OTC 3
Transaction in WinGold
Staff raises sale in WinGold: product, quantity, customer record. WinGold applies live pricing. Payment received and recorded. Stock auto-deducted from branch balance.
WinGold
OTC 4
Item Retrieval & Handover
Items retrieved from secure storage. Verified against WinGold sale record. Receipt generated from WinGold. Minted bars: assay card must be intact at handover.
Staff
OTC 5
End-of-Day Reconciliation
Branch manager runs WinGold daily sales report. Physical stock vs WinGold closing balance reconciled. Cash/payment reconciliation signed off. Discrepancies escalated immediately.
Daily
🛒 E-Commerce — Online Order Fulfilment (Phase 1)
EC 1
Order Placed on Website
Customer places order. WinGold records as pending sale. Live stock and pricing pulled from WinGold. Customer sees availability by location (Ainslie-style). Order confirmation auto-sent.
WinGold Auto
EC 2
KYC & AML Check (GBG)
KYC triggered via GBG integration. New customers: ID verification required. Order held pending clearance — items soft-reserved. Orders failing KYC are cancelled; reservation released.
Compliance
EC 3
Order Allocation & Routing
KYC cleared → WinGold allocates order to optimal dispatch location. Phase 1: routed to Chermside or nearest branch. Stock hard-reserved — removed from website display.
WinGold
EC 4
Packing & Dispatch
Staff retrieve items, verify against order. Secure packaging (plain/unmarked). Delivery: courier booked, tracking recorded in WinGold. Pickup: customer notified for collection.
Staff
EC 5
Order Completion
Delivery: WinGold marked DISPATCHED on courier booking. Pickup: marked FULFILLED on collection (ID re-verified). Stock finalised. Customer receives confirmation.
WinGold Auto
PHASE 1 DISPATCH: All e-commerce orders dispatched from Chermside (head office) and branches only. Factory-based dispatch introduced in Phase 2.

PROCESS 4 Scrap Gold & Jewellery Processing

⚖️ Scrap Gold Purchase — Branch
S1
Customer Arrives with Items
Customer presents gold items (jewellery, scrap, coins, bars) for sale. Staff explains valuation process.
S2
KYC & AML Check (GBG)
KYC check completed via Gold Secure GBG before any assessment begins. Proceeds only on KYC clearance.
Compliance
S3
Gold Testing & Valuation
Acid test / XRF analyser for purity. Items weighed on calibrated scales. WinGold calculates offer using live spot price, purity, and backend slabs. Offer presented to customer.
WinGold Pricing
S4
Contract & Payment
Customer accepts → sale contract created in WinGold. Customer signs contract. Payment made. Items logged as scrap/buyback inventory at branch, pending factory assessment.
WinGold
🏭 Factory Assessment & Sorting
F1
Transfer to Factory
Scrap items batched and transferred from branch to factory. WinGold transfer record created (same process as internal stock transfers).
WinGold Transfer
F2
Factory Assessment
Each item: purity tested, weighed, condition assessed. Sorted into: re-saleable jewellery · gold for refining/casting · scrap. Results recorded against each item.
Factory Staff
F3
Sorted Items — Back to Inventory
Re-saleable jewellery → transferred to Chermside for e-commerce (Phase 3) or eBay (interim). Gold for refining → retained at factory for casting. Scrap → sent to refiner. All movements in WinGold.
3 Streams
F4
Jewellery Sales — eBay (Interim)
Until Phase 3: re-saleable jewellery photographed and listed on eBay. Sales tracked manually. Revenue reconciled with eBay payouts in accounting. WinGold jewellery category in Phase 3.
Interim Process
SORTED GOLD FOR REFINING → RE-ENTERS PROCUREMENT PROCESS AS CAST/MINTED BARS
PHASE 3 TARGET: Jewellery will have a dedicated WinGold category and website section (alongside bullion, buybacks, and collectibles). eBay continues as secondary channel but inventory tracked in WinGold as master system. Note: WinGold is primarily a bullion system — jewellery implementation requires careful planning.

CONTROLS Stock Counting & Reconciliation Schedule

📅 Daily — Transaction Reconciliation
WinGold transaction log reconciled against physical opening/closing stock. Cash/payment reconciliation completed. Signed off by branch manager each end of day.
📊 Weekly — Spot Check
Random selection of product categories at each branch. Any variances investigated same day. Covers all locations on a rotating basis.
🔎 Monthly — Full Stock Count
All items at all locations counted by two staff members. WinGold variance report reviewed. Discrepancies reported to management within 24 hours. Formal sign-off required.
STOCK AGING: Stock aging feature not currently available in WinGold. To be developed post go-live — either within WinGold or as a separate module. Decision to be confirmed with Goran.
