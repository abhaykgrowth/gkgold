System Integration — WooCommerce vs WinGold Role Split

🛒 WooCommerce — Order Journey System
• Full order fulfilment & status tracking (source of truth for order journey)
• Customer-facing status updates at every stage
• Email template triggers at each order status change
• AusPost tracking integration (via plugin + API)
• All 3 fulfilment paths managed here (Delivery / Pickup / Storage)
• Staff update status: In Progress → Dispatched → Delivered / Collected
💎 WinGold — Metal & Accounting System
• Transactional and accounting system for metal inventory
• Only needs to know: (1) Pickup vs Delivery method, (2) When metal is delivered/collected
• "Dispatched" in WooCommerce = "Metal Delivery Done" in WinGold
• "Collected" in WooCommerce = "Metal Delivery Done" in WinGold
• Storage: customer metal account, storage records, billing schedule
• Future: WooCommerce "Out for Delivery" auto-triggers WinGold metal delivery
Staff Dual-Update Requirement: For all delivery and pickup orders, staff must currently update both WooCommerce (order status) AND WinGold (metal delivery) manually. Future automation planned once WinGold API endpoints are confirmed.

KEY RULES: Insurance = 0.5% admin charge (NOT 'insurance' on invoice until lawyer validates). Orders over $100,000 = hand-to-hand delivery. ALWAYS Express Post + Signature Required. NEVER standard post. Payment window = 24 hrs customer / 26 hrs staff. Market Loss Policy applies on cancellation. Order ID as payment reference = MANDATORY.
FREE STORAGE — 3 TYPES: (1) Pool Allocated (Free) — 3-month complimentary period on every purchase, e-commerce and counter. (2) Un-serialised — GoldSecure branded items, lower fee after free period. (3) Serialised — all other (non-GoldSecure) products, higher fee, ring-fenced. Fees sent quarterly with metal holdings statement. Requires WinGold ledger configuration by Palani + customer portal build.
BAILMENT AGREEMENT — ONE-TIME SIGN + APPENDIX MODEL: The Bailor-Bailee Agreement is signed once only by the customer. Every subsequent purchase or trade that includes storage does not require a new agreement — instead, a new Appendix is generated and appended to the master agreement, capturing the details of that transaction (item, storage type, free period dates). The portal must store the master agreement + all appendices in one place and maintain a version history per customer.
STORAGE PORTAL REQUIREMENT: A dual-portal is needed: (1) Admin Portal — for staff to manage customer storage holdings, view master agreements + all appendices, trigger billing, handle withdrawals. (2) Customer Portal — for customers to view metal held, current value, storage records, and access their agreement + appendix history. WinGold is the backend; portal frontend to be built as part of the broader customer portal project.
